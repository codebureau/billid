# Clients

The client list on the left side of the app is your starting point. Each client is a separate consulting engagement with its own billing configuration.

---

## Adding a client

1. Click **+ Add** in the top-left panel
2. Enter the client's **name** (required) and **company name**
3. The client appears in the list immediately
4. Go to the **Settings** tab to fill in billing details

---

## Client details

Each client has four tabs:

| Tab | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Timesheet** | All work entries for this client |
| **Invoices** | All invoice records |
| **Summary** | Hours, revenue, and billing status |
| **Settings** | Contact details and billing configuration |

---

## Searching clients

Type in the search box above the client list to filter by name or company.

---

## Deleting a client

Click **Delete client** in the top-right of the client detail area. You will be asked to confirm before the client and all associated data is permanently removed.

!!! warning
    Deleting a client removes all their work entries, invoices, and settings. This cannot be undone.
