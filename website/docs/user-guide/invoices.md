# Invoices

The **Invoices** tab shows all invoice records for a client, including which work entries were included and the total value.

---

## Invoice records

Invoices in Billable are records — they represent invoices you have raised in your external accounting or invoicing tool (Xero, MYOB, FreshBooks, etc.). Billable does not generate invoice documents.

Each invoice record stores:

- Invoice date
- Total hours and amount
- Grouped invoice lines (by work category)
- The individual work entries included

---

## Viewing an invoice

Click any row in the invoices list to see:

- The invoice lines (grouped summary)
- The individual work entries that were included

---

## How invoices are created

Invoices are created from the **Timesheet** tab via **Prepare Invoice**. See [Timesheet → Preparing an invoice](timesheet.md#preparing-an-invoice).

---

## Invoice status on work entries

Once an entry is invoiced, it is marked in the timesheet and excluded from future invoice preparation. The invoice number or reference is stored against each entry.
